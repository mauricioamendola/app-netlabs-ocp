About Us

For the last 22 years we have been driving the Internetworking and Operating System revolution. Now we are looking forward to expand this vision into the Quantum Computing realm. We're specially interested in the intersection between Operating Systems, Networking and Quantum Computing.

We are a boutique company focused in the design and research of advanced technologies, with 22 years of experience in the development of customized solutions for different types of mobile operators, government, financial institutions, retail, and IT companies in the local and international market.

Thanks to our degree of involvement in Linux, Big Data and our focus on permanent research, Red Hat Inc and Cloudera have selected Netlabs as their main partner in its region, to implement the proofs of concept of their most recent technologies.

Agility, expertise, low operating costs, great track record, international technical support and the ability to establish strategic alliances are all differential factors that ensure our success in the industry.
